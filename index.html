a late night in Atlanta

it's a late night in Atlanta
i have that indescribable feeling cities always bring me
there are so many lives here, having different experiences but all at the same time, in nearly the same place
it feels so connected, but also so lonely
but it's nice in a way? maybe "beautiful" would be a better word for it
the light's gone out in the apartment i was paying attention to
with the tv playing, well idk
that building looks like an apartment building, with all the different colored lights
the building next to it has the identical standard, vauge yellow/white color lights, maybe an office building?
i wonder why they keep the lights on, maybe to keep away the monsters we all fear
that without the lights and movement, we might be caught by that lonliness we try so hard to avoid

sometimes i like to try and sit with that silence, that lack of anything
it undoes something, and sits on your chest, and moves up into your breath
at some points, it feels like everything you've ever know is happening all at once

and then the movement and noise is back, and it leaves, but it's never far away
